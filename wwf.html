Role: Creative & Art Director

A visual fight against poaching of wild animals that wants to raise awareness among the public through a totally interactive advertising of various pieces, where the user must feel that is the protagonist at all time.

The solution of using a set of advertising pieces distributed through posters, oppis and videos, linked to each other through an app, creates an a closed campaign.

In order to get the attention of the user, it is necessary to create a visual content that creates some sensation inside. In these case it consists in posters which try to seek attention in different ways as a puzzles in oppis and scanning posters around the streets with videos inside. The slogan next to the name of the #breakthebubble campaign combines perfectly as it represents the humanity that lives locked in its own bubble and does not see or does not want to see the reality that is before their eyes.
