Role: Art Director & User Experience Lead

The proposal was a challenge, converting the online purchase of clothes into something more dynamic and close to the user. As a fictitious brand, there was not a great development but even so, it has a potential future for the experience that the user would receive as feedback in that kind of eShops. For this purpose, this this conceptual idea was proposed as an interact between the user and the digital platform being able to see throw an interactive video the sets of clothes that they would have access to buy all in order to go beyond of the sample photographs.

At the same time, the UI and UX were worked on to create a new concept of less intrusive eShops, with a more clean, clear and minimalist interface.
